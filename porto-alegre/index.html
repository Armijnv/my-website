Frequently asked questions

About doing business in Porto Alegre with local support.

Do I need to speak Portuguese to do business in Porto Alegre?
English is not widely spoken in business contexts in Porto Alegre outside of large multinationals. Having a local host who speaks both English and Portuguese fluently is a significant advantage in meetings, negotiations, and day-to-day logistics.
What industries are active in Porto Alegre?
Porto Alegre and the surrounding Rio Grande do Sul state are strong in agribusiness, manufacturing, footwear, petrochemicals, and IT services. It's one of Brazil's most important economic hubs in the south.
How far in advance should I book a host?
We recommend reaching out at least one week before your arrival so we can properly prepare your itinerary, logistics, and accommodation. That said, we handle last-minute requests when capacity allows — just message us on WhatsApp.
Can my host join supplier meetings or factory visits?
Yes. That's one of the core services. Your host can attend meetings as a bilingual business support professional, help you interpret what's being said, and assist with follow-up communication in Portuguese.
Is accommodation included in the host service?
Accommodation is a separate service. We have vetted apartments in Porto Alegre that you can book alongside your host package — or independently if you prefer.
